Portugal recorded a government debt equivalent to 117.70 percent of the country's Gross Domestic Product in 2019.

Government Debt to GDP in Portugal averaged 80.80 percent from 1990 until 2019, reaching an all time high of 131.50 percent in 2016 and a record low of 50.30 percent in 2000. This page provides - Portugal Government Debt To GDP - actual values, historical data, forecast, chart, statistics, economic calendar and news. Portugal Government Debt to GDP - values, historical data and charts - was last updated on August of 2020.

Government Debt to GDP in Portugal is expected to reach 132.00 percent by the end of 2020, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Portugal Government Debt to GDP is projected to trend around 124.00 percent in 2021 and 118.00 percent in 2022, according to our econometric models.
